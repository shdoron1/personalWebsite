I am a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Pennsylvania's sociology department. My Current project investigates facets of urban inequality perpetuated by different experiences of home ownership and the home rental market. I explore these questions through quantitative data, including GIS and spatial analysis, as well as qualitative ethnographic methods.

Pasts projects include an investigation of the Israel/ West Bank barrier route and the history and trajectory of Community Land Trusts (CLTs) in urban America.
